What is the Unchaind Alliance?

We support a blockchain-free technology stack.

We believe in a decentralized web that is based on protocols and interoperability.

We know from practical experience that there are very few technology problems for which a blockchain is the best answer.

We acknowledge the current climate crisis as a fact, and value reduction of atmospheric carbon over the fetishization of tax avoidance.

We have witnessed the destruction of sanity that arises when social agendas are tightly-coupled to virtual currency.

We acknowledge that innovations in, and democratization of, contract law require a relationship with, and recourse to, an appropriate legal jurisdiction.

We have no appetite for further growth in the tulip market.

How can I join the coin-free movement?

Embed the logos of the Unchaind Alliance on your web applications, and include a link to https://unchaind.org.
